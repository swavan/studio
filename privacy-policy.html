Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 21, 2025
Last Updated: December 21, 2025

Important: Swavan SSH Studio is designed with privacy as a top priority. Your SSH credentials, passwords, and private keys are stored locally on your device using encrypted storage. We do NOT transmit your sensitive data to any servers or third parties.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Swavan SSH Studio ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Swavan SSH Studio mobile application (the "App").

By using Swavan SSH Studio, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you use Swavan SSH Studio, you provide the following information:

  • SSH Connection Details: Server hostnames or IP addresses, port numbers, connection names, and descriptions
  • SSH Credentials: Usernames, passwords, SSH private keys, and passphrases for encrypted keys
  • Connection Settings: Custom landing paths, connection preferences, and configuration settings
  • Vault Password: A master password used to encrypt and protect your stored connections

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect limited technical information:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and app version
  • Crash Reports: Technical diagnostic information if the app crashes (no personal data included)
  • Usage Statistics: Anonymous app usage patterns to improve performance (if you opt-in)

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect or track your browsing history
  • We do NOT access your contacts, photos, or other personal files
  • We do NOT collect location data
  • We do NOT use cookies or tracking technologies

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected solely for the following purposes:

  • Establish SSH Connections: To connect you to your remote servers using the credentials you provide
  • Store Connection Profiles: To save your connection settings for quick access in the future
  • Secure Your Data: To encrypt and protect your credentials using your vault password
  • Improve the App: To fix bugs, improve performance, and add new features
  • Provide Support: To respond to your support requests and technical issues

4. How We Store and Protect Your Information

4.1 Local Storage Only

All your SSH credentials and connection details are stored exclusively on your device. We do NOT upload, sync, or transmit your sensitive information to our servers or any cloud services.

4.2 Encryption and Security Measures

  • AES Encryption: All connection data is encrypted using industry-standard AES encryption
  • Vault Protection: Your vault is locked with a master password that only you know
  • No Cloud Backup: Sensitive credentials are excluded from iCloud backup to prevent unauthorized access

4.3 SSH Security

When establishing SSH connections:

  • All communications are encrypted using SSH protocol (industry standard)
  • Private keys never leave your device
  • Passwords are transmitted only over encrypted SSH connections
  • We support SSH key-based authentication for enhanced security

5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 We Do NOT Share Your Data

We do NOT sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information or SSH credentials with any third parties, including:

  • Advertisers or marketing companies
  • Analytics services (beyond anonymous crash reporting)
  • Data brokers or aggregators
  • Social media platforms
  • Other apps or services

5.2 Legal Requirements

Since all data is stored locally on your device and we do not have access to your credentials or connection details, we cannot disclose information we do not possess. In the unlikely event we are required by law to provide information, we can only provide non-sensitive technical data such as app version information.

6. Your Data Rights and Control

6.1 Access Your Data

All your data is accessible within the app. You can view and edit your connection profiles at any time.

6.2 Delete Your Data

You have complete control over your data and can delete it at any time by:

  • Deleting individual connections within the app
  • Clearing all app data through the app settings
  • Uninstalling the app (removes all locally stored data)

7. Data Retention

Your data is retained on your device only as long as:

  • You keep the app installed on your device
  • You have not deleted specific connections or cleared app data

When you uninstall Swavan SSH Studio, all locally stored data is permanently deleted from your device.

8. Children's Privacy

Swavan SSH Studio is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will take steps to delete such information.

9. International Data Transfers

Since all your data is stored locally on your device, there are no international data transfers. Your SSH connections to remote servers are direct connections from your device and are not routed through our servers.

10. Third-Party Services

Swavan SSH Studio may contain links to third-party websites or services (such as documentation or support pages). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.
